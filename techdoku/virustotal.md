## Verwendung mit CLI

```
vt scan <url>

vt analysis Njk2MzBlNDU3NGVjNjc5ODIzOWIwOTFjZGE0M2RjYTA6MTcxMzc3OTM5OA==
vt analysis Njk2MzBlNDU3NGVjNjc5ODIzOWIwOTFjZGE0M2RjYTA6MTcxMzc3OTM5OA== --include=_id,_type,**.category,**.result
```
